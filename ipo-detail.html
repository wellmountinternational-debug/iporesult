How to Check Allotment
  1. Select the IPO you applied for from the dropdown
  2. Choose PAN Number (most common) or Application Number
  3. Enter your details and click Check Allotment
  4. You'll be redirected to the official registrar
  5. If allotted, shares are credited to DEMAT on allotment date. Refunds processed within 2 business days.
