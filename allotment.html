📋 How to Check IPO Allotment Status

1
Select the IPO from the dropdown above that you applied for.
2
Choose your check method — PAN Number (most common), Application Number, or DP/Client ID.
3
Enter your details — your PAN is linked to your DEMAT account and IPO application.
4
Click Check — you'll be directed to the official registrar (KFin or Link Intime) for the result.
5
If allotted, shares are credited on the allotment date. Refunds are processed within 2 business days if not allotted.
