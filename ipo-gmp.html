📖 What is GMP?

GMP
Price at which IPO shares trade in the unofficial grey market before listing on BSE/NSE.
Kostak Rate
Amount paid for an entire IPO application, regardless of whether you get allotment.
Est. Listing
Issue Price + GMP = Estimated Listing Price. Informal estimate — actual listing may differ.
