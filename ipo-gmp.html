📖 What is GMP (Grey Market Premium)?

GMP
The price at which IPO shares are traded in the unofficial grey market before listing on exchange.
Kostak Rate
The price paid for an entire IPO application (not per share). Paid regardless of whether you get allotment.
Estimated Listing
Issue Price + GMP = Estimated Listing Price. This is an informal estimate, actual listing may differ.
